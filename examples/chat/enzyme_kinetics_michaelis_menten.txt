 ===== L (Logic) =====

 Nonlinear MM fit logic:
 - Model: v = (Vmax * S) / (Km + S)
 - Estimate (Vmax, Km) per experiment via robust least squares
 - Compute quality (R², RMSE)
 - 95% CI via simple bootstrap (B≈300)
[*logic_fit] #0


 Meta-analysis logic:
 - Aggregate (Vmax, Km) across experiments with fixed effects (weight = 1/SE²)
 - Compute pooled standard errors
[*logic_meta] #0


 ===== R (Result) – Execution =====

   Apply *logic_fit to *data_assays[i] and produce:
     parameters (Vmax_i, Km_i)[*params_i] #0
     quality (R2_i, RMSE_i) [*fit_quality_i] #0
     95% confidence intervals [*ci_i] #0
     residual diagnostics [*residuals_i] #0
 Consolidate *params_* with *logic_meta [*meta_params] #0
